Pesto Sauce Recipe

Description

Italian sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 bunch fresh basil leaves
  • 1 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 2 ounces cream cheese
  • Â¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 large clove garlic, minced

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
  2. Add the basil leaves and cook until tender and bright green, 2 to 5 minutes.
  3. Drain basil, reserving 2 tablespoons of the basil water.
  4. Blend basil, 2 tablespoons basil water, Parmesan cheese, cream cheese, olive oil, butter, and garlic in a blender until smooth and creamy.
